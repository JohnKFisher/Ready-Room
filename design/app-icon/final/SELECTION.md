# Approved Icon Selection

Winner: `Glass Dashboard Tile`, refined into `ready-room-final`

Why this won:

- it reads most clearly at small sizes because the silhouette is dominated by two large glass panels rather than many small details
- it most directly matches the app's dashboard-plus-briefing purpose
- the accent rail echoes the in-app audience-color system without turning the whole icon into a rainbow
- it feels closest to a native macOS utility icon rather than a generic mail or home app mark
